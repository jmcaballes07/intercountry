Yes! We are now in CEBU!

With 25 years of experience in the design and deployment of distance and face-to-face training, Intercountry meets all the training needs of its customers through a range of 5 training methods. Each of these modalities is used for the added value it brings: courses with or without e-learning, workshops, coaching, immersion and conversations. Intercountry offers an integrated multimodal remote service worldwide and face-to-face in France and Spain. Intercountry trains in English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Arabic, Russian, Chinese, Japanese and Italian.

France
Intercountry is present throughout France thanks to a team of more than 200 trainers.

Europe
Intercountry is also present in Spain, England, and Ireland with around 120 trainers.

In the world
Intercountry is also present in the United States, Africa, Australia and Asia with around 40 trainers.
